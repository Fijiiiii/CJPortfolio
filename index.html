Framework

Unstructured OJT usually involves a new employee shadowing one or several skilled employees,
who oversee the trainee’s job tasks and various other day-to-day activities. There are no real
milestones to hit, and the plans can be customized at will depending on the trainee’s experience and
abilities. The company only require OJT students to their company with a deep understanding of system
development and the basic knowledge of programming languages.

Self Assessment

My experience with the individuals in my OJT virtual workplace.

I was actually worried because this would be my first job in my chosen field, and I hate messing up anything. I was at first intimidated, but once I had the opportunity to work with them virtually, it was a very enjoyable experience for me because they had already treated me as one of their own. My boss regularly tells jokes and tells funny stories to lighten the mood of our team. He always tells me that coding doesn't feel like work if you enjoy it, so I became heavily involved in development from that day forward.

What technical knowledge have I gained from the company?

Because of the things I have learned and obtained from my experience working there, this company has been a significant steppingstone in my career as a full stack developer. They taught me that teamwork makes development more enjoyable and efficient. Interacting with them has broadened my experience and changed my perspective on development.

OJT EXPERIENCE HIGHLIGHT:

The most memorable experience that i have encountered during my training in the company is how i have completed my very own onformative website for a client of our company, it is really hard to forget al the first in our lives.

The most memorable event/experience during my internship

The most memorable experience I had during my internship was being given the opportunity to make some changes and add some features to the CHSI Philippines website. I was nervous at first because of my limited skills, but my team, particularly my supervisor, guided me to do something I didn't even know I could do, and for that I am very truly thankful.

What suggestions do I have for improving the company's and university's OJT programs?

The pandemic, in my opinion, severely limits a student's learning experience. It is extremely difficult to learn virtually, especially when it comes to job experience, because certain meetings must be met in person, which is difficult due to the restriction of face to face. I sincerely hope that arrangements can be made between the school and the company to make it at least a hybrid OJT program in which the student can attend a specific event or meeting in person when necessary.

My advice to those who will be starting OJT soon...

My only advice to those starting OJT soon is to do an advance study on the given field you are in since this will save you a huge amount of time during the pressure of working in a company which will give a good impression to the advisor you have.
